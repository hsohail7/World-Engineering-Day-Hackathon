Our Solutions and Ideas

Here are some key solutions and ideas we are implementing to address the extreme heat problem in refugee camps:

  • Enhanced shelter design to provide cooling and safety.
  • Rainwater collection and filtration system for clean water.
  • Solar power utilization to provide sustainable energy.
  • Environmentally friendly materials prioritizing durability and comfort.
  • Adaptability and flexibility in shelter design to meet diverse needs.
  • Cost-effective solutions to ensure affordability.
